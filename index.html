01 — About

I think most products fail at the obvious step: actually talking to the people who'll use them. Not surveys — sitting with someone while they're confused and watching what they reach for.

I do my best work before things are figured out — when nobody's sure what to build and the question itself might be wrong. I build side projects when something bothers me enough. I use AI heavily, not because it's trendy but because it genuinely changes what one person can do.

I'm moving into product management deliberately. The BA title is where I started; PM is where I'm heading.
